Specialized Spare Parts Procurement & Port Management

When the right part needs to be at the right place at the right time.

Downtimes cost money – especially when a ship is waiting for a specific component or technical equipment. MareSphere supports you in procuring critical spare parts quickly, targetedly, and with an eye on quality and reliability, and organizes the port call so that operational business is minimally affected.

Specialized Spare Parts Search:

Support in identifying and selecting suitable components, especially for hard-to-find or obsolete parts.

Coordination of Offers and Ordering:

Support in obtaining, evaluating, and selecting offers, as well as in coordination with suppliers and service providers.

Port-related Coordination:

Accompanying organization around the port call in connection with part delivery, e.g., coordination with technicians, shipyard, or local service partners.

Tracking and Documentation:

Transparent communication on status, support with inquiries, and orderly filing of relevant documents for later verification.

Your advantage: You receive independent, practical support that understands your technical requirements and structures communication between shipping company, suppliers, and service providers.

Shipping connects. We help ensure the right part and the right people come together at the right time – so your ship stays in motion.
